About Prestige PR

Building brands, shaping narratives, and driving influence for over two decades. We are the strategic communication partner that global brands trust when reputation matters most.

Our Story

2002 - The Beginning

Founded by Sarah Johnson in a small Manhattan office with a vision to revolutionize how brands communicate with their audiences. Started with just 3 clients and a passion for storytelling.

2008 - Expansion

Opened offices in London and Hong Kong, establishing our global footprint. Successfully navigated the financial crisis for our clients, proving our crisis management expertise.

2015 - Digital Transformation

Launched our digital PR division, embracing social media and influencer marketing. Became the first agency to offer AI-powered sentiment analysis to clients.

2020 - Crisis Leadership

Guided over 100 brands through the global pandemic with innovative communication strategies. Recognized as Crisis Management Agency of the Year.

2024 - The Future

Now serving 500+ clients across 6 continents with 200+ professionals. Continuing to innovate with AI-driven insights and real-time media monitoring.
